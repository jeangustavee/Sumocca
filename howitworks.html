FAQ

What do i need in order to set up support on my site?

After you have selected and purchased your support plan, we will request your login info so we can collect some details about your site. This helps us connect to your site in order to install our services offered (i.e., security monitoring, backups, and plugins). Our staff keeps these credentials private and will never share these details with anyone not associated with SUMOCCA.

What if I don't need monthly support?

Chances are you probably do need monthly support. If nothing else we're an extra level of assurance to protect your website, but if all you really need is quick help for a single task, we can just do it for you for a hour based fee.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Mastercard, Visa, Discover, and American Express via Paypal. Paypal is the safest way to pay. After signup, cards are auto-charged each month on a recurring basis until you decide to cancel or upgrade your plan. 

Does it matter which webhost I use?

We definitely recommend working with a quality web host, but we’ll be happy to work with whoever you’ve chosen.

Am I locked into a contract? 

No. We don’t use shady cell phone provider tactics here. We believe in earning your business month after month, and you can cancel your service with us at any time, risk free.

How many support requests can I submit each month?

You can ask us as many questions as you’d like, and if your request is covered by your site management plan, then we’ll take care of it right away. There’s no limit to the number of tasks we perform as long as it’s covered by your plan. Sometimes we’ll refer you to an article or send you a quick answer of our own. If your support request isn’t covered by your plan, we’ll give you an estimate for completing the work you need done, or can upgrade you to a plan with more services. In short, send us a note whenever you think we can make your life easier.
